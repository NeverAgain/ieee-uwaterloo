Beginning of Term General Meeting

Where: Engineering 5 Room 2004
When: Tuesday September 27th, at 5:30pm

Come on out to the Beginning of Term general meeting. Learn about IEEE and about the IEEE Student Branch here at the University of Waterloo. Find out what exciting events we have planned, and how you can be a part of them. Sign up for one of our design teams, and learn how you can volunteer to take a leadership role within the branch. Free food will be provided.

International IEEE Xtreme Programming Competition 5.0

When: October 22nd

What: Think you're a good programmer? Like challenges? Don't like sleep? Sign up with up to two other students as a team in the International IEEE Xtreme Programming Competition which is a 24 hour long programming competition. Prizes for the top international teams include trips, netbooks, and more. Regional winners also receive prizes from IEEE.



Student Branch Robotics Design Competition and Workshops

When: October - November

What: Ever wanted to learn about electronics, microcontrollers, or robots? Want to get hands on skills using Arduinos and coding in C? Want to compete against other students' robots at the end of the month? This is the event for you. Don't worry if you've never worked with electronics or robots before, we take you through it step by step with weekly workshops teaching you the hands on skills you'll need to build a robot as per the design competition.



IEEE Xplore Tips n Tricks Training

Where: Davis Center Library Room 1568

When: October 11th, 11:30am-12:30pm

What: A fast paced 60 minute session about using the IEEE Xplore website. Learn how to use the website to find jobs, technical papers, and more. Prizes and food provided.
